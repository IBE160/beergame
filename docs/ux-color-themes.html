Color Distinction

These four colors are distinguishable for users with color vision deficiencies (tested for deuteranopia and protanopia). Each role also uses shape/label identification in addition to color.

Design Philosophy

This color system balances calm professionalism (educational context) with engaging energy (game experience). The calm blue primary creates trust and focus, while teal accents add excitement for key actions. Semantic colors provide instant feedback, and role colors make multi-role charts immediately scannable.

60/30/10 Rule Applied: Neutrals (backgrounds, text) dominate at 60%, primary blue appears consistently at 30% (navigation, charts, borders), and teal accent highlights critical actions at 10% (CTAs, important notifications).
